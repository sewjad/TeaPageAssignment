TEA EXPORTING COUNTRIES

Beautiful Tea Plantation in Sri Lanka

Some of the main targets for Ceylon Tea as recorded are Russian Federation, Turkey, the Middle East, Europe, African Region, United states, Japan, Australia and China. Being the leading single country destination for Ceylon Tea, The Russian Federation accounts for 14% (US$ 228.3 Mn) of Sri Lankan Tea exports. Turkey has turned out to be the second largest market absorbing nearly 13% of overall tea exports (US$ 209 Mn) in year 2014 recording an yearly growth of 40% compared to year 2013. Around 34% of the total Sri Lankan Tea exports were destined to the Middle East region, in year 2014. The main export destinations were Iran, Iraq, UAE, Syria, Kuwait, Jordan & Lebanon. The European and African regions respectively have accounted for 8.8% and 6.3 % of the total tea exports. The main tragets were Germany, Netherlands, Poland, Finland, Belgium, UK, Italy & France within the Europe and countries such as Libya, Egypt, South Africa and Angola in the African region. Tea exporters in Sri Lankan were also able to earn considerable amount of foreign exchange by providing to the single export markets such as Japan (3.1%), Australia (2%), USA (2.0%) and China (1.6%).
